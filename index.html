About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-focused organization that aims to prepare individuals for the growing challenges of cyber, technology, and digital risk management. It focuses on building practical knowledge by combining academic learning with real-world industry experience. Through collaborations with universities, professionals, and organizations, IDR provides training, research opportunities, and advisory support in areas such as cybersecurity, artificial intelligence governance, and technology risk. The institute aims to develop skilled professionals who can understand, manage, and reduce digital risks in modern organizations and rapidly evolving technological environments.
